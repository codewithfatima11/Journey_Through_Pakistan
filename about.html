About Journey through Pakistan

We highlight Pakistan’s culture, landscapes and travel opportunities. This project is built to showcase places, curated activities, and travel resources — created by Fatima Mirza as a portfolio and learning project.

Who we are

Journey through Pakistan is an initiative to showcase the country’s hidden gems, culture, and natural beauty. We curate trips, recommend local guides, and publish travel tips to help visitors plan safe, meaningful journeys.

Note: This is a demo/portfolio project (not a licensed travel agency). Booking actions are simulated unless you add a backend integration.
